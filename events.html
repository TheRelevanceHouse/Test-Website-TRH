RELEVANT PEOPLE, RELEVANT IDEAS
Why? If you don’t believe in yourself, why would anyone else believe in you? An assured, compelling pitch is crucial for attracting investment and gaining traction in the market. Whether you’ve just had the eureka moment of a great business idea, or are aiming to scale your business to reach its full potential, you need to prepare the perfect pitch for your brand.

What?

Want to learn how to convincingly pitch ideas to relevant people such as investors, clients or the media? Think you’re already good enough? Pitch your idea to our panel of communications pros and get immediate feedback on your messaging, body language and delivery. Quick-fire pitches from a variety of emerging tech firms keep things fun and interesting.

Who?

This event is open to all startups, entrepreneurs and founders willing to test their ideas in a fun and open-minded environment. The relaxed atmosphere makes it perfect for anyone wishing to enjoy an after-work drink while developing their skills, learning and expanding their network.

When & Where?

On the last Thursday of each month at the Pirate Hub, Swisscom Blockchain, Müllerstrasse 16, Zürich.

Pitch us your idea on the 23rd of April (06:30 PM CET).
